Game Sypnosis
You are the last survivor of the infamous armadillo race. You were born different with a defect that made your skin green. However this defect enabled you to survive from a strange illness that wiped out everyone except you. While roaming the galaxies you found a mysterious fruit that can turn your skin back to normal and prolong your lifespan.

However these fruits will only appear once every 500 years and for a minute each time. These fruits are special. They appear one at a time and the next only appears after you have finished eating one. Eat as much as you can before they disappear in 60 seconds!!

Game Controls
W -> Move up
S -> Move down
A -> Move left
D -> Move right
Q -> Rotate CCW
E -> Rotate CW

Game Score: 0

Elapsed Time: 0/60 seconds
